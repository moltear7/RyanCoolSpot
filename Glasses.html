Glassware

My sophomore year at the Cleveland Institute of Art I began working with glass. I found myself immediately drawn to the fluidity of the material fascinating as well as the technical aspect of the practice. Being a purely sculpture student I had never experienced such a practice of craft in my art making, and I found myself and continue to find myself enraptured by the study of glass sculpting.
