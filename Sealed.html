Sealed

Beginning with a study of the dybbuk box, Sealed is a piece that exists at an intersection between multiple cultural practices. Shinto ofuda, names of God in Hebrew, and the Daoist realm of immortals are represented in this piece. The names of God written on the ofuda, Yahweh and El-Roi mean Lord of Lords and Lord of Sight respectively, invoke the sight of God himself onto this object. Seen through a hole in the piece itself is a mountain range. This mountain range represents the Daoist ideal, a realm of mental peace and serenity.
